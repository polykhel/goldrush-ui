Revenue Report

Generate revenue reports for a specific date range

Report Information

This report provides detailed information about revenue during the selected period, including:

  • Total revenue for the selected period
  • Revenue breakdown by booking
  • Revenue breakdown by payment method
  • Revenue breakdown by service type

How to use

  1. Select a date range using the filter panel
  2. Choose your preferred export format (PDF or Excel)
  3. Click "Generate Report" to download the report
